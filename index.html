Rules

For this world cup game, everyone will have to pick 12 teams. The world cup divides teams up into 12 groups, and 4 pots. The groups are the groups that your teams will be playing against in the group stage. The pots are to separate teams by their skill, teams in pot 1 are generally better and teams in pot 4 are generally worse. Each team is only in 1 group and 1 pot. What pot a team is in is next to the team name. You will be picking one team in each group, and 3 from each pot.

You will get points based on how each team does in each game they play. The further your teams go in the World Cup, i.e. the more games they play, the more opportunities to score points you will have. The scoring is the normal way soccer teams get points:

  • 3 points for a win
  • 1 point for a draw
  • 0 points for a loss

Additionally, you can get points through awards that FIFA hands out:

  • 1 point if your team has the player who wins the Golden Boot award (most goals scored)
  • 1 point if your team has the player who wins the Golden Ball award (best overall player)
  • 1 point if your team has the player who wins the Golden Glove award (best goalkeeper)
  • 1 point if your team has the player who wins the Best Young Player award
  • 1 point if your team has the player who wins the FIFA Fair Play award
